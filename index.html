Key Insights & Q2 Priorities
💡 Revenue Beat — But Fee Size Needs Attention
Leverage Homes closed Q1 at $1,223,012 — 111% of the $1.1M target. However the average fee of $37,061 came in below the $40K goal. 13 of 33 deals closed same-quarter (15.12%), meaning 20 deals were sourced in prior quarters. The pipeline entering Q2 is $1,353,000 — healthy but dependent on UW and disposition execution. Call Center remains the dominant ARIP source at 50%.
🔴
Call Center at 40% of target (1,043 vs 2,600). Despite being Q1's #1 deal source, volume was cut more than in half. This is a direct risk to Q2 and Q3 ARIP volume. Prioritize dialer, list, and headcount review immediately.
⚠
Virtual show rate at 61.9% vs 73% target — 417 no-shows on 2,291 appointments. Each missed virtual = ~$37K in potential fee. A 10-point improvement in show rate = ~229 additional attended appointments per quarter.
🔴
21 VP-missed appointments. Per SOP, this should be zero. These are motivated sellers who had confirmed time with a closer. Implement hard calendar rules for in-person buffering and same-day confirmations from AMs.
🔴
62 late UW events — 5× the quarterly cap. Avg speed worsened from 23 hrs in January to 70 hrs by late March. VPs heading into appointments without complete UW is a direct conversion drag. Enforce 18-hr UW escalation in Q2.
⚠
Assignment disposition averaging ~89 days vs a 45-day target. Begin buyer outreach at deal review entry, not exit. Same-day pre-marketing cuts this cycle significantly.
✅
Pull-through hit 70.93% — exactly on target. Follow-up appointments at 207% of goal (833 vs 403). In-person at 127% of goal (380 vs 300). The follow-up and IP machines are working.
🎯 Q2 Priorities
1. Call Center Volume → 200+/wk. Audit dialer, list freshness, caller headcount.

2. Avg Fee Size → Push toward $40K+. Focus on higher-ICP properties and novation / fix-flip mix (already 50% of ARIP).

3. UW SLA → 18-hr escalation trigger. Weekly late-UW audit per underwriter.

4. Virtual Show Rate → Automated SMS + email at 24 hrs and 1 hr before. Target 73%+.

5. Q2 Pipeline ($1.35M) → Prioritize the 37 deals in Under Contract. Compress ARIP and disposition cycles to accelerate collection.
